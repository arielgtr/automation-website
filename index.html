About Us

Digitalisation is revolutionizing industrial practices in all professional sectors. Many schools, Colleges and universities are facing the challenge of transmitting the know-how of industries. In order to support them in their path to industries, Automation Service Center Ltd has developed certain programs linked to practical teaching in our Training Center and created a concept of support service in this area.

The Automation Service Center Ltd is a company established in Rwanda, which aims to bring technology to the front at different levels, and also considering ways to increase productivity, through technology and improve the knowledge of people who works and use it. It is in this context that Company undertakes to provide a variety of practical training in: Automation, Electricity, Plumbing and Welding including support services: Automation solutions, Consultancy, troubleshooting, Installations and Maintenance.
